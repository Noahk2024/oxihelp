A doctor in your hands

With heart disease on the rise, we've developed Helping Hearts to ensure EVERYONE has access to premiere medical diagnoses tools, even from their home. Using novel ML algorithms paired with government research, Helping Hearts quickly and accurately diagnoses you with numerous heart diseases based on multiple data points.

Meet our sources.

We've focused on using only the most up to date cardiovascular health datasets from the best sources. Below is the data used to train our models and the research papers used to base preliminary knowledge on:
